Our Story

ChurBro was created in 2024 by Dr. Zohaib Rana, a Postdoctoral Fellow in RNA & Cancer Therapeutics at the University of Otago's Department of Biochemistry.

As a researcher working with computational biology and bioinformatics, Zohaib noticed students and families in Dunedin were wasting money by not comparing prices across supermarkets. What started as a weekend project quickly grew into a tool used by hundreds of Dunedin residents every week.

How ChurBro Works

1. Automated Daily Scraping (10 PM Every Night)

Our automated scrapers collect:

  • Product names and descriptions
  • Current prices (including Club Deals and specials)
  • Original prices (for calculating savings)
  • Unit prices (per kg or per item)

2. Intelligent Price Matching

ChurBro's algorithms match identical products across stores by analyzing product size, type, and brand.

3. Cross-Store Comparison

When you view a product, you'll see prices at other stores and potential savings by shopping elsewhere.
