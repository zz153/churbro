Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ChurBro

What is ChurBro?
ChurBro is a free meat price comparison website for Dunedin. We compare prices across New World, PAK'nSAVE, Woolworths, and Mad Butcher daily so you can find the best deals.
Is ChurBro really free?
Yes! 100% free. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no paywalls. We're supported by advertising.
How often are prices updated?
Prices are updated daily. We regularly collect fresh pricing data from all four stores to ensure you have access to current deals and specials.
Are the prices accurate?
We strive for 100% accuracy, but please note:
  • Prices reflect the most recent data collection
  • Stores may change prices throughout the day
  • Some products may become unavailable after our update
  • Always verify prices at the store before purchasing
ChurBro helps you identify the best deals, but final prices should always be confirmed in-store.
Do you include Club Card prices?
Yes! We display New World Club Deal prices and all special offers from all stores.
Which stores does ChurBro track?
Currently:
  • New World City Centre (~340 products)
  • PAK'nSAVE Dunedin (~389 products)
  • Woolworths Central Dunedin (~271 products)
  • Mad Butcher Dunedin (~112 products)
We're constantly working to expand our product range!
Why can't I find a specific product?
Not all products are available at all stores, and some items may be out of stock or discontinued. We only show products that are currently listed on each store's website. Try searching with different keywords or check back later!
Can I search for specific brands?
Yes! Our search works with product names, brands, and types. Try searching for "Pams bacon," "Hellers sausages," or just "chicken breast."
Will you add more stores or products?
We're exploring expanding to more grocery categories (vegetables, dairy, pantry items) and potentially more stores. If there's demand from the Dunedin community, we'll keep growing! Share your feedback via our contact page.
How do you make money?
ChurBro is currently supported by Google AdSense advertising. We're a Dunedin-based startup experiment built to help the local community save money on groceries.
Who built ChurBro?
ChurBro was built by a local Dunedin researcher and radio host who got tired of overpaying for meat! It's a small startup project designed to help the Dunedin community. Learn more on our About page.
I found a bug or incorrect price. How do I report it?
We appreciate your help! Please contact us with details about the issue, and we'll fix it as quickly as possible.
