At ChurBro, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit churbro.net.

TL;DR: We collect minimal data (only your email if you subscribe), use cookies for analytics, and never sell your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Email Address: If you subscribe to our weekly deals newsletter
  • Feedback: If you contact us via email

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, device type
  • Cookies: Small files for site functionality
  • IP Address: For analytics (anonymized)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Display meat price comparisons
  • Send weekly email newsletters (if subscribed)
  • Improve website functionality
  • Respond to your inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

Purpose: Website analytics
Data: Anonymous usage statistics
Opt-out: Google Analytics Opt-out

Google AdSense

Purpose: Display advertisements
Data: Cookie data for ad personalization
Control: Google Ad Settings

Mailchimp

Purpose: Email newsletter delivery
Data: Email address
Control: Unsubscribe link in every email

4. Cookies

ChurBro uses cookies for:

  • Essential: Site functionality and user preferences
  • Analytics: Understanding website usage
  • Advertising: Displaying relevant ads

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data
  • Secure hosting
  • Limited access to personal data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Unsubscribe from emails anytime
  • Opt-out of analytics

Contact us at contact@churbro.net to exercise these rights.

7. Children's Privacy

ChurBro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
